About Me

Hello! I'm Ryen, a first-year BSIT student at Far Eastern University - Institute of Technology. I'm just beginning my journey into the world of programming and technology, driven by my fascination with the possibilities that coding offers.

What drew me to Information Technology is the incredible flexibility it provides—the opportunity to work with computers whether onsite or from the comfort of home. I'm passionate about building solutions and connecting with people through technology.

Currently, I'm learning the fundamentals of programming languages like C++, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I value dedication and attention to detail in everything I do.

Outside of studying, I enjoy binge-watching series and anime, listening to music, playing mobile games, and watching nature documentaries. Back in my province, I love riding my motorcycle around the island—it lifts my spirit and gives me a sense of freedom.

I also cherish spending quality time with friends. While I'm still discovering my specific path in IT, I'm excited about building things and collaborating with others to grow and learn.

My goal is simple: to develop the skills I need to create a stable, fulfilling career. I hope to afford my own place, build my dream workspace, provide for myself, and save for the future.
